Privacy Policy

GAU Admission Office Feedback System

Grand Asian University Sialkot — Admission Office

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

1. Overview

The GAU Admission Office Feedback System (“the App”) is a kiosk-based application developed for Grand Asian University Sialkot to collect visitor feedback, admission queries, and issue reports within the Admission Office environment.

This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects user information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User-Submitted Information

  • Admission queries (name, category, message)
  • Staff feedback (ratings and comments)
  • Issue reports (description, urgency level)

2.2 System-Generated Data

  • Auto-generated token numbers (e.g., TKN-YYYY-XXXX)
  • Timestamp of submissions
  • Submission status updates

2.3 Device & Usage Data

  • Device type (tablet/kiosk mode)
  • App performance logs
  • Connectivity status (online/offline)

3. How We Use Information

  • Managing admission office queries
  • Improving staff performance and service quality
  • Tracking reported issues and resolutions
  • Generating internal reports and analytics
  • Ensuring operational efficiency of the admission office

4. Data Storage

All data is securely stored in Google Firebase (Firestore Database). Offline data is temporarily stored using Hive local storage and synchronized automatically when internet connectivity is restored.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties.

Data is only accessible to authorized university administration staff and system administrators of GAU Admission Office.

6. Data Security

  • Firebase Authentication for admin access
  • Firestore Security Rules
  • Role-based access control
  • Secure token-based system design

7. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as necessary for operational and academic purposes. Admins may delete or archive records when required.

8. Offline Functionality

The app supports offline usage using local storage (Hive). Data is automatically synced when internet is restored.

9. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected inside the application or official documentation.
